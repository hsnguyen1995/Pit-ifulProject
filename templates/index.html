Commercial Avocado Production Around the World (2017)
The visualization to the left maps countries that reported commercial production of avocados to the United Nations in 2017.
Addtionally, countries that also reported growing the follow: Tomatoes, Garlic, Onion, Cilantro, & Limes are noted in green.
Note that this visualization is not limited to the production of Hass Avocados, but include other varieties of avocados.
How much do these Pit-iful fruits cost!?
This time series plot shows the average price of a SINGLE avocado. I repeat, SINGLE avocado. That's insane. The California plot seems to show wild fluctuations in price. Ultimately, the average cost has been increasing over the years. Select different regions to see if there are similar trends. How could the price be possibly so high? How many avocados are being produced???
Looking at the plot for California, the plot also fluctuates but not as badly. The beginning and end of the plot are within the same range which means the overall volume has been steady. So it looks like volume isn't as much of a factor in the increase of avocado price. At least for California it isn't.
